    About Us

    TrojanX Pty.Ltd is a fast-growing global application and web-development company based in Brisbane Australia. We pursue to forever provide the highest quality of service possible.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is simple, which is to provide people an affordable and effective means of making their business idea come to lie within the digital era, and in turn transforming their lives through the creation of wealth. The client is - and will - always be the most important person in our eyes.

    Our Plan

    At TrojanX we value:
    - Truth
    - Integrity
    - Customer centred service (excellent customer experience)
    - Accountability
    - Reliability & Responsibility

    Our Vision

    We specialize in providing highly personalised applications and websites to suit the business goals of all our clients - additionally providing business consulting services to ensure that clients understand the most effective and efficient method of them monetising their project and achieving their business goals.
